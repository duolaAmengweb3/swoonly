Frequently asked

How much is it? Browsing and the first chapters of every book are free. Swoonly Pro is $5.99/month or $39.99/year with a 7-day free trial, and unlocks unlimited reading.

Are there coins? No. Never. Pro unlocks everything for one price.

How do I cancel? Settings app → your name → Subscriptions → Swoonly → Cancel. You keep access until the period ends.

Restore purchases? Settings → Restore purchases inside Swoonly.

Where do the stories come from? They are original fiction created with AI, for entertainment.
