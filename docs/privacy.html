Swoonly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-04

Swoonly is designed to be private by default. We do not require an account, and we do not track you.

What we collect

Nothing personal. Your reading progress, saved stories, and preferences are stored only on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data, and the app contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Purchases

Swoonly Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We never see your payment details. Subscription status is verified on-device through Apple’s StoreKit.

Content

All stories in Swoonly are original works of fiction created with AI, provided for entertainment. They do not depict real people or events.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reading reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. No data leaves your phone.

Children

Swoonly is rated for older teens and adults and is not directed to children under 13.
